**✅ Panduan Lengkap Detail Fisik Karakter untuk AI Image Generation**

Berikut adalah **list panduan** yang lengkap dan terstruktur. Kamu bisa copy-paste sesuai kebutuhan.

### 1. Bentuk Tubuh (Body Shape / Body Type)

| Kategori              | Sebutan yang Bagus (Recommended)                              |
|-----------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------|
| Umum                  | hourglass figure, curvy, voluptuous, athletic, slim thick    |
| Sangat Melengkung     | extreme hourglass, pear-shaped, bottom-heavy, top-heavy      |
| Dewasa & Menggoda     | mature voluptuous body, seductive proportions, fertile body  |
| Langsing + Berisi     | slim thick, toned curvy, athletic curvy                       |
| Berotot               | toned body, muscular female, fit athletic body                |
| Ramping               | slender, petite, delicate frame                               |

**Kombinasi kuat**: `voluptuous hourglass figure, mature seductive body`

### 2. Payudara (Breasts)

**Ukuran / Volume:**
- Small / Modest: small breasts, petite breasts, perky breasts
- Medium: medium breasts, full breasts, rounded breasts
- Large: large breasts, big breasts, heavy breasts
- Very Large: very large breasts, massive breasts, huge breasts, enormous breasts
- Ekstrem: gigantic breasts, hyper breasts, colossal breasts

**Bentuk & Tekstur:**
- Shape: round breasts, teardrop breasts, perky breasts, heavy hanging breasts, firm breasts
- Lainnya: deep cleavage, sideboob, underboob, breast expansion, soft breasts, jiggly breasts

**Kombinasi terbaik**: `large heavy breasts, full and round, deep cleavage`

### 3. Pinggang (Waist)

| Tingkat                 | Deskripsi yang Efektif                              |
|-------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------|
| Sangat Langsing         | slim waist, narrow waist, tiny waist, wasp waist   |
| Sedang                  | defined waist, toned waist                          |
| Kontras Tinggi          | extremely narrow waist, dramatic waist-to-hip ratio |

**Kombinasi kuat**: `slim narrow waist, dramatic waist-to-hip ratio`

### 4. Panggul & Bokong (Hips & Buttocks)

**Panggul (Hips):**
- wide hips, very wide hips, broad hips, childbearing hips, flared hips
- extreme wide hips, massive hips

**Bokong (Butt):**
- plump ass, round ass, thick ass, juicy ass, bubble butt
- huge ass, massive ass, fat ass, firm round ass, heart-shaped ass
- thick juicy thighs + wide hips (biasanya dipasangkan)

**Kombinasi Terbaik**: `very wide hips, thick juicy thighs, plump firm round ass`

### 5. Tinggi Badan (Height)

| Tingkat          | Deskripsi Prompt                                      |
|------------------|-------------------------------------------------------|
| Pendek           | shortstack, petite woman, 150cm tall                  |
| Sedang           | average height, 160-165cm tall                        |
| Tinggi           | tall woman, 170cm tall, leggy woman                   |
| Sangat Tinggi    | very tall woman, 175-180cm tall, statuesque           |
| Ekstra Tinggi    | towering beauty, 185cm+ tall                          |

**Rekomendasi tambahan**: `tall elegant woman, long legs`

### 6. Keseluruhan Proporsi & Aura Tubuh

**Deskripsi Keseluruhan yang Kuat:**
- perfect anatomy, flawless proportions, seductive body, idealized female form
- beautiful feminine curves, fertile body, goddess-like proportions
- thick curvy body, thicc, super proportioned
- elegant mature body, toned yet soft, athletic yet voluptuous

### Contoh Penggunaan Full Body Description (siap copy)

```
voluptuous hourglass figure, mature seductive body, large heavy round breasts, extremely narrow waist, very wide hips, thick juicy thighs, plump firm heart-shaped ass, long toned legs, tall elegant woman (172cm), perfect anatomy, seductive proportions, flawless skin
```

---

**Tips Penggunaan Prompt:**
- Urutan yang bagus: **Bentuk tubuh → Payudara → Pinggang → Panggul & Bokong → Kaki → Keseluruhan**
- Semakin spesifik = semakin baik, tapi jangan terlalu berlebihan (bisa bikin AI bingung).
- Gunakan kata **"very", "extremely", "massive", "plump"** untuk memperkuat.
- Untuk karakter seperti Retsu Unohana, biasanya cocok dengan: **mature voluptuous + tall + hourglass**.

**✅ Panduan Lengkap Warna Rambut & Deskripsi Rambut untuk Karakter Anime Perempuan**

### 1. Warna Rambut Dunia Nyata yang Bisa Diaplikasikan ke Anime

#### Warna Alami (Natural Hair Colors)
- **Hitam** → Jet black, raven black, obsidian black, deep black, pitch black
- **Cokelat** → Dark brown, chestnut brown, chocolate brown, light brown, caramel brown, ash brown, golden brown
- **Blonde** → Platinum blonde, golden blonde, honey blonde, ash blonde, strawberry blonde, dirty blonde
- **Merah** → Auburn, copper red, ginger red, wine red, fiery red, mahogany
- **Abu-abu / Putih** → Silver gray, platinum white, snowy white, salt & pepper (untuk karakter tua/mature)

#### Warna Dyed / Highlighted (Masih Realistis)
- Ash blonde, rose gold, balayage brown, ombre black-to-brown, burgundy, chocolate cherry, caramel highlights

**Catatan**: Warna-warna di atas sangat bagus untuk karakter anime yang ingin terlihat lebih “realistic” atau semi-realistis, meskipun tetap di dunia anime.

---

### 2. Panjang Rambut (Hair Length)

| Panjang              | Deskripsi Prompt yang Efektif                              |
|----------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------|
| Pendek               | short hair, bob cut, chin length hair                     |
| Sedang               | shoulder length hair, medium length hair                  |
| Panjang              | long hair, waist length hair, hip length hair             |
| Sangat Panjang       | very long hair, floor length hair, thigh length hair      |
| Extreme              | ankle length hair, extremely long flowing hair            |

**Contoh**: `waist-length jet black hair`, `thigh-length silky silver hair`

---

### 3. Gaya Rambut (Hairstyles)

**Gaya Umum Populer di Anime:**
- Straight hair, slightly wavy hair, wavy hair, curly hair, loose curls, ringlets
- Twin tails (odango, pigtails), ponytail, high ponytail, low ponytail, side ponytail
- Bun, messy bun, braided hair, French braid, fishtail braid
- Hime cut (princess cut), layered hair, shaggy hair, wolf cut
- Slicked back, side swept, hair over one eye, messy hair

**Kombinasi Kuat**:
- `long straight black hair with slight waves`
- `flowing waist-length wavy hair`
- `neat hime cut with long straight hair`

---

### 4. Poni / Bangs (Fringe)

| Jenis Poni                  | Deskripsi Prompt                                      |
|-----------------------------|-------------------------------------------------------|
| Poni Lurus Biasa            | straight bangs, blunt bangs, full bangs               |
| Poni Signature Unohana      | straight bangs, long straight bangs covering forehead |
| Side Bangs                  | side-swept bangs, asymmetrical bangs                  |
| Poni Terpisah               | parted bangs, curtain bangs                           |
| Poni Pendek                 | short bangs, micro bangs                              |
| Kombinasi                   | long bangs framing face, hair strands framing face    |

**Framing Strands** (sangat penting untuk karakter cantik):
- `two long strands framing face`, `face-framing sidelocks`, `long bangs with side strands`

---

### 5. Karakteristik & Tekstur Rambut Lainnya

**Tekstur & Kualitas:**
- Silky hair, glossy hair, shiny hair, smooth hair, lustrous hair
- Soft hair, fluffy hair, voluminous hair, thick hair, fine hair
- Flowing hair, dynamic hair, hair with movement

**Detail Tambahan:**
- Hair ornament: ribbon, hair clip, hairpin, flower hairpin, headband
- Wet hair, messy hair, windblown hair, floating hair
- Color gradient: black to blue gradient, ombre hair
- Shine effect: beautiful hair highlights, reflective hair

---

### Contoh Prompt Rambut Siap Pakai

**Untuk Retsu Unohana Style:**
```
long flowing jet-black hair, signature straight blunt bangs, two long elegant strands framing her face, silky smooth lustrous hair, slight natural waves at the ends, glossy highlights, perfect detailed hair strands
```

**Versi Lain:**
- `waist-length platinum blonde hair with curtain bangs and face-framing strands, silky and shiny`
- `thigh-length deep auburn wavy hair, side-swept bangs, voluminous and fluffy`
- `shoulder-length messy silver hair with layered cut and choppy bangs`

---

### Struktur Prompt Rambut yang Optimal (Urutan Terbaik)

1. Panjang + Warna  
2. Tekstur / Kualitas  
3. Gaya utama  
4. Poni + Face-framing  
5. Detail tambahan (movement, shine, ornament)

**Contoh Full Hair Prompt:**
```
extremely long waist-length jet black hair, silky glossy smooth texture, straight with soft waves at the tips, signature long straight bangs, two long strands elegantly framing her face, lustrous highlights, beautiful detailed hair strands, flowing naturally
```
